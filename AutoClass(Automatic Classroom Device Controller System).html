How it works

One loop, from sensor to log

Every cycle of the system follows the same path — the same flow you'll see live on the dashboard.

01

Sense

PIR, DHT22, and microwave sensors stream motion, temperature, and humidity from the room.

02

Decide

The system processes readings in real time and checks whether the room is occupied.

03

Act

Appliances switch on while the room is in use and power down after a no-motion timer.

04

Log

Every action is recorded and saved to the database for the history page.
